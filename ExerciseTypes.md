# English Grammar Practice – Exercise Types

---

## 1. Sentence Correction (Fix My Mistakes)

I give you incorrect English sentences → you correct them.

**Example:**  
❌ He don’t likes go to gym.  
✔️ He doesn’t like going to the gym.

Good for: grammar accuracy, verb forms, common mistakes.

---

## 2. Natural-Sounding Rewrite

You rewrite correct sentences to sound more natural.

**Example:**  
Original: I very like this idea.  
Natural: I really like this idea.

Good for: sounding natural and fluent.

---

## 3. Fill-in-the-Blanks

You fill in missing words (verbs, prepositions, articles, etc.)

**Example:**  
I haven't seen him \_\_\_ Monday.

Good for: prepositions, articles, tenses.

---

## 4. Choose the Correct Option (Multiple Choice)

I give options → you choose the correct one.

**Example:**  
Which is correct?  
a) I didn’t went  
b) I didn’t go  
c) I don’t went

Good for: quick grammar testing.

---

## 5. Transform the Sentence

You rewrite a sentence using a different structure.

**Example:**  
Make it passive: Someone stole my phone.  
→ My phone was stolen.

Good for: tenses, conditionals, passives, indirect speech.

---

## 6. Translation (Ukrainian → English)

Translate sentences to practice structure and vocabulary.

---

## 7. Mini Writing Tasks

Write 2–4 sentences about a topic.

Good for: flowing, natural writing.

---

## 8. Spot-the-Mistake

You identify which part of the sentence is incorrect.

**Example:**  
She go to work by car every days.

Good for: grammar awareness.

---

## 9. Vocabulary Expansion

I give you a word → you write 2–3 sentences using it.

---

## 10. Mixed Challenge

A mix of multiple exercise types in one session.

---

## 11. Word Scramble (Build the Sentence)

I give you words in a random order → you arrange them into a correct, natural sentence.

**Example:**  
Words: _yesterday / I / to the gym / went_  
Correct sentence: _I went to the gym yesterday._
